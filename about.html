About Simmons Energy Corporation

Simmons Energy was founded to provide dependable, engineer-led energy systems tailored to the needs of industrial customers. We focus on quality, safety, and serviceability in everything we build.

Our approach

  • Consultation — Evaluate site needs, regulatory constraints, and operational goals.
  • Design & Engineering — Develop cost-effective, maintainable system designs with clear documentation.
  • Fabrication — In-house and partnered manufacturing to ensure quality and schedule adherence.
  • Commissioning & Support — On-site commissioning and long-term service contracts to keep systems running.
